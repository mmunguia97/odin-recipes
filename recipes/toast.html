Avocado Toast

Andy Li, CC0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Hungry and don't have a lot of time? Avocado toast is the answer!

Easy, quick, and delicious. What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pitted avocado
  • 1 slice of whole-grain bread
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread in a toaster or toaster oven.
  2. Mash the avocado.
  3. Spread mashed avocado on top of toast.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
