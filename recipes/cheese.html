Grilled Cheese Recipe

Inside-out Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Mack Male from Edmonton, AB, Canada, CC BY-SA 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

This inside-out grilled cheese sandwich is a twist on a classic recipe!

For the cheese lovers. Cheesy on the inside. Cheesy on the outside.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1/2 cup of shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Melt 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter on a non-stick pan over medium-low heat. Place bread slices over hot butter.
  2. Spread 1/4 cup of cheese on 1 bread slice. Place other bread slice, butter side up, on top of cheese.
  3. Spread some of the remaining cheese on top of of sandwich.
  4. Melt the remaining butter and flip sandwich onto hot butter. Spread remaining cheese on sandwich.
  5. Cook until cheese on bottom is crispy. Flip sandwich and repeat.
