Pepperoni Pizza

MVS9966, CC BY 3.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

A classic and easy to follow pepperoni pizza recipe!

Ingredients

Pizza Sauce

  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 12 ounces of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups of all-purpose flower
  • 1/4 ounce of yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 1/3 cups of hot water
  • 1/3 cup of oil

Toppings

  • 1 cup of shredded mozarella cheese
  • 6 ounces of sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F and prepare two 12-inch pans.
  2. Make the sauce: Combine water, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Make the crust: Combine 2 cups of flower, yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix with water and oil. Gradually add remaining flour until dough forms.
  4. Knead dough for around 4 minutes until it's smooth and elastic.
  5. Divide dough in half and place each piece on prepared pans.
  6. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  7. Bake for approximately 20 minutes or until ready.
